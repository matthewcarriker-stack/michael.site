About Michael

Passion, Flavor & Purpose

Michael Galyean is a multi-talented professional who has built a remarkable career at the intersection of culinary mastery, powerful storytelling, and a magnetic public presence. With over fifteen years of hands-on experience, Michael brings an unmatched level of dedication and craft to every engagement.

From intimate private dining experiences crafted with farm-to-table precision, to electrifying keynote presentations that leave audiences motivated and transformed — Michael's work is driven by a singular belief: every moment is an opportunity to inspire.

His celebrity appeal and warm charisma make him the ideal addition to high-profile events, brand activations, media appearances, and more. Michael doesn't just show up — he elevates the room.
